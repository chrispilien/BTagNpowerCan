Professional Development

To prepare students for internship and job placement, we simulate a professional work environment by requiring and coaching students to:

  • Dress in professional business clothing 2-3 days/week throughout the program
  • Communicate with professionalism in person and via email
  • Attend class on time every day – we are very strict about attendance and punctuality
  • Follow through on assigned tasks, complete labs and homework, and show up for appointments with staff


    • Weekly Professional Development training includes:

  • Identifying skills, interests and accomplishments and creating an IT specific resume
  • Networking, personal branding and social media
  • Delivering effective presentations, business writing, teamwork, managing conflict at work
  • Professionalism in the workplace, and more!

    To be considered for graduation from our program students must:
  • Demonstrate professionalism and respect
  • Complete all weeks of in-class training
  • Write the certification exams
  • Attend all classes, appointments and complete homework
  • Complete an 8-12 week paid internship or equivalent

    • Once you graduate, NPower Canada will assist in placing you into employment. After you are employed, we will continue to support you for two years through career coaching, follow-up, events, mentorship and networking supports.
